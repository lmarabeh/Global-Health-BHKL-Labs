Overview

Welcome to BHKL Labs' study on global health trends and key development indicators. This project aims to provide insights into various trends of countries around the world through an interactable visualization, with the initial project being a focus on the creation of a composite index development score to reflect correlations in human geography (through life expectancy and GDP per capita).

Key Features

Interactive Year Slider

Dynamic slider that updates the visualization in the range from 2012 to 2021.

Zoom Controls

Zoom in and out of the chart with updated scales, includes reset button.

Bubble Tooltips

Hover over countries to view detailed statistics including GDP per capita, life expectancy, total population, and aggregate scores.

Color Scale

Visual representation of aggregate score results.

Bubble Size

Size of plot points represented by a country's total population.
